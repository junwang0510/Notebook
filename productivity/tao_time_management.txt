Terence Tao: on time management

source: https://terrytao.wordpress.com/2008/08/07/on-time-management/
date: 2024/11/19

- often work on multiple joint papers and a single individual paper
- adjust tasks based on the fluctuating productivity
- batch-process low-focus tasks
- try to divide a large task into smaller tasks
- learn skills that are highly repeatable
- break rules seldomly
